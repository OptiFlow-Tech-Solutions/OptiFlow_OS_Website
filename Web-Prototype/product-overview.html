Everything You Need To Run Operations

Spotlight on the 6 modules MSMEs use most. Every module is built for Indian business workflows.

Task Management

Create, assign, track, and verify every task. Each task has an owner, deadline, priority, and verifier. No task falls through cracks.

Outcome: Zero missed deliverables. Every task tracked to completion.

Attendance

Digital check-in/check-out with location tagging and face verification. Real-time attendance dashboards for every department.

Outcome: Eliminate manual registers. Accurate payroll in one click.

SOP Library

Document every business process as a standard operating procedure. Version-controlled, searchable, with training records.

Outcome: Zero dependency on key employees. Processes survive people.

Reports & Analytics

Executive dashboards with real-time metrics. Task completion rates, department performance, attendance trends, audit trails.

Outcome: Data-driven decisions. Know exactly what's happening across the business.

Worklists

Pre-built task templates for recurring operations. Daily production checklists, shift handover lists, quality inspection workflows.

Outcome: Standardise recurring work. 80% fewer checklist errors.

Checklist Management

Create, assign, and verify checklists for production, quality, safety, and maintenance. Photo proof capture, timestamped verification.

Outcome: 98% checklist compliance. Audit-ready records always available.

Leave Management

Apply, approve, and track leaves with balance visibility. Auto-deduct from attendance. Team calendar for planning.

Outcome: Zero leave disputes. Automated balance tracking.

Training

Assign SOP-based training modules to employees. Track completion, quiz scores, and re-training schedules.

Outcome: Every employee trained on their role. Compliance records always current.

Inventory

Track raw materials, finished goods, and consumables. Low-stock alerts, consumption trends, supplier management.

Outcome: 15% lower inventory carrying costs. Never run out of critical stock.

Meetings

Schedule, track attendance, document minutes, and assign action items. Every meeting drives accountability.

Outcome: Meetings produce tasks, not just talk. 100% action-item follow-through.

Verification

Multi-level task and checklist verification. Photo proof, timestamped sign-offs, escalation on rejection.

Outcome: Quality assurance built into every process. 90% fewer quality escapes.

Notifications

In-app, email, and SMS alerts for assignments, deadlines, approvals, and escalations. Never miss a follow-up.

Outcome: Zero missed deadlines from forgotten tasks. Automated reminders for every stakeholder.
