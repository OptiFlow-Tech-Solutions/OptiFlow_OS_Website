# PAGE-002: Problem & Solutions

## Summary

The Problem & Solutions page is a narrative-driven landing page that diagnoses the 6 biggest operational challenges Indian MSMEs face daily — from WhatsApp chaos to Excel version hell — and positions OptiFlow OS as the single source of truth that replaces manual chaos with structured, process-driven systems.

## Why

MSMEs don't know they have a "systems problem." They think missed follow-ups, delayed approvals, and lost tasks are normal. This page must make the invisible cost visible — showing ₹ impact, hours lost, and growth ceilings — before introducing OptiFlow OS as the solution.

## Capabilities

1. **Animated Pain-Point Rotator** — Auto-cycling carousel of 8 industry-specific pain points (textile, manufacturing, trading, logistics) that reinforces the visitor's own experience with `pp-accent` highlighting.

2. **Operational Chaos Map** — Visual flow diagram showing chaos sources (WhatsApp, Excel, calls, registers, memos, sticky notes) funneling into a state of "CHAOS" and resolving into "OptiFlow OS — Single Source of Truth."

3. **Industry-Specific Problem Cards** — 6 cards with real scenarios, industry tags, ₹ impact tags, and ROI-fix lines covering textile, manufacturing, trading, and logistics verticals.

4. **WhatsApp Chat Mockup** — Simulated chat group showing operations chaos with real-looking message patterns, paired with 3 cost-of-chaos cards (tasks get lost, no audit trail, zero visibility).

5. **Cost of Doing Nothing Stats Panel** — Three stat blocks quantifying annual revenue loss (₹5-20L), monthly hours wasted (60-100), and owner time spent on follow-ups (40%).

6. **Solution Flow Diagram** — Linear flow (Chaos → OptiFlow OS → Process-Driven Business) with module tags showing the 8 platform capabilities.

7. **People-Driven vs Process-Driven Comparison** — Side-by-side grid contrasting 7 attributes of people-driven (dependency, errors, delays, hours lost, risk, blind, unsustainable) vs process-driven operations.

8. **Before/After Compact Comparison** — Two-column before/after grid with 7 operational dimensions showing the transformation.
